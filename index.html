About World Puzzle Battle

World Puzzle Battle is a global platform where puzzle enthusiasts compete in speed and accuracy. Unlike traditional jigsaw puzzles, our dynamic slide-and-swap mechanism tests your visual memory and reflexes. Every day, a new "Today's Challenge" image is released worldwide, ensuring a fair competition ground for all players.

ğŸ® How to Play & Scoring

Select your difficulty level and click on puzzle pieces to swap them. Your goal is to restore the original image as quickly as possible.

  • Difficulty Multipliers: We reward ambition. Playing on Hard (8x8) grants significantly more points (15x multiplier) than Easy mode.
  • Today's Challenge: Participating in the daily event gives you a +50% Score Bonus. It's the best way to climb the leaderboard!
  • Speed Matters: The faster you complete the puzzle, the higher your base score.
